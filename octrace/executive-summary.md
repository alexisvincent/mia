Production-ready European B2B intelligence platform with **40 million company records**, intelligent data processing infrastructure, and AI-ready capabilities.

1. **Data Asset**: 40M European company records with continuous updates from ~20 data sources
2. **Matching & Trust System**: Proprietary algorithms for intelligent conflict resolution and merge strategies
3. **Data Provenance**: Cell-level tracking of source and update history for full audit trails
4. **Modern Infrastructure**: NestJS/TypeScript on Kubernetes with event-driven architecture (BullMQ, PostgreSQL, Redis)
5. **Agentic Framework (In development)**: AI agents for autonomous research and data enrichment (in development)

- **Cloud-agnostic**: Kubernetes deployment portable to any provider
