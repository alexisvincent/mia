# LOPS System Overview

**LOPS** = LEX OPS (LEX = ALEXIS)

A highly systematized GTD-based methodology designed to maximize personal productivity by capturing work, executing efficiently, and building unshakeable trust in the system.

## GTD Foundation

Getting Things Done (GTD) is a productivity methodology by David Allen designed to create 100% trust in a system for collecting tasks, ideas, and projects.

**The Core Insight:** Your brain is terrible at remembering things at the right timeâ€”it creates an incessant feeling of "I need to remember... something" instead of reminding you when you actually need to act.

**The Solution:** Offload your brain by capturing everything into a trusted external system. When your system and your trust in it are solid, your subconsciousness stops tracking tasks and frees up mental energy for productive thinking and creativity.

**Key Principle:** Convert vague thoughts into clear, physical, visible actions. This reduces resistance to doing things because the "pre-processing" is already done.

LOPS adapts GTD principles to work within Linear and with AI assistance. For a comprehensive overview of traditional GTD, see [gtd-summary.md](./gtd-summary.md).
