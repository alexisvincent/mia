# LOPS System Overview

**LOPS** = LEX OPS (LEX = ALEXIS)

A highly systematized GTD-based methodology designed to maximize personal productivity by capturing work, executing efficiently, and building unshakeable trust in the system.

## GTD Summary

Getting Things Done (GTD) is a productivity methodology by David Allen that uses a simple system of capturing all tasks and ideas, clarifying what each one means and what action is needed, organizing everything by context and priority, and then engaging confidently with your work knowing nothing will be forgotten. For a detailed overview of how GTD typically works, see [gtd-summary.md](./gtd-summary.md).

## Core Workflows

### 1. Capture (Ongoing)
- Extract thoughts, tasks, and reference items from your brain into the system
- Multi-channel capture: AI assistant, WhatsApp, voice input, etc.
- Automated ingestion: Reading messages and suggesting items for capture
- Goal: Get everything out of your head

### 2. Triage (Daily, 15-30 min)
- Process inbox items through GTD's clarify & organize steps
- Assign metadata (labels, contexts, due dates)
- Quick-win rule: Complete items taking <2 minutes immediately
- Output: Organized, actionable items ready for prioritization

### 3. Prioritize (Daily & Weekly)
- Daily: Select 3-5 items for today from This Week list
- Weekly: Move 10-15 items from Backlog â†’ This Week
- Review incomplete items from previous day/week
- Goal: Keep focus tight and realistic

### 4. Review (Weekly)
- Cleanup and maintenance tasks
- Prune Someday/Maybe list
- Review system health and trust
- Realign with goals
