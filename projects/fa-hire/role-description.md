# Founders Associate Role Description

## Position Overview
The Founders Associate is a hybrid role that combines the tactical support of an Executive Assistant with the strategic thinking of a Chief of Staff. This position serves as a direct force multiplier for the Founder (Lex), operating at the intersection of high-level strategic initiatives and day-to-day operational excellence.

## Primary Purpose
**Force Multiplier**: The core mission is to amplify the founder's impact by taking ownership of tasks, projects, and responsibilities that free up founder time for highest-value activities.

## Role Positioning
- **More Senior Than**: Executive Assistant
- **More Junior Than**: Chief of Staff
- **Sweet Spot**: Strategic tactical executor with broad scope and autonomy

## Key Responsibilities

### Executive Support (25%)
- **Calendar Management**: Optimize scheduling, manage priorities, coordinate meetings
- **Travel Coordination**: Book flights, hotels, ground transportation, manage itineraries
- **Communication Management**: Email triage, response drafting, stakeholder coordination
- **Meeting Support**: Agenda preparation, note-taking, follow-up coordination

### Personal Support (10%)
- **Lifestyle Management**: Personal appointments, reservations, event planning
- **Gift Sourcing**: Research and procurement of personal and business gifts
- **Personal Admin**: Handle personal tasks, errands, and administrative needs
- **Family Support**: Coordinate family-related activities and logistics as needed

### Strategic Project Support (35%)
- **Project Coordination**: Manage cross-functional initiatives and strategic projects
- **Research & Analysis**: Conduct market research, competitive analysis, business intelligence
- **Process Improvement**: Identify and implement operational efficiencies
- **Stakeholder Management**: Interface with key partners, vendors, and team members
- **Special Projects**: Lead ad-hoc initiatives requiring strategic thinking and execution

### Company Operations (25%)
- **Administrative Support**: Handle company-related paperwork and processes
- **Vendor Management**: Coordinate with service providers and suppliers
- **Team Coordination**: Support internal team communication and logistics
- **Documentation**: Maintain records, processes, and organizational knowledge

## Success Metrics
- **Time Savings**: Measurable reduction in founder's time spent on operational tasks
- **Project Delivery**: Successful completion of strategic initiatives on time and budget
- **Quality of Support**: High-quality output requiring minimal founder oversight
- **Proactive Problem-Solving**: Anticipating needs and solving problems before they escalate
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction**: Positive feedback from internal and external stakeholders

## Ideal Candidate Profile

### Required Skills
- **Executive Presence**: Comfortable interfacing with high-level stakeholders
- **Project Management**: Proven ability to manage complex, multi-stakeholder projects
- **Communication**: Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- **Problem-Solving**: Strong analytical and creative problem-solving abilities
- **Technology Proficiency**: Comfortable with modern productivity and collaboration tools
- **Discretion**: Ability to handle confidential information with complete professionalism

### Preferred Experience
- 3-5 years in executive support, consulting, or project management roles
- Experience in startup or high-growth environments
- Background in business operations or strategy
- Previous experience supporting C-level executives

### Personal Attributes
- **Highly Organized**: Systems-oriented with attention to detail
- **Adaptable**: Thrives in dynamic, fast-paced environments
- **Proactive**: Takes initiative and anticipates needs
- **Resourceful**: Finds creative solutions to complex challenges
- **Trustworthy**: Maintains confidentiality and represents the founder professionally
- **Cultural Fit**: Aligns with company values and working style

## Working Relationship
- **Direct Report**: Reports directly to Founder (Lex)
- **Autonomy Level**: High degree of independence with clear communication expectations
- **Decision Authority**: Empowered to make decisions within defined parameters
- **Interaction Style**: Regular check-ins, clear priority setting, outcome-focused

## Career Development Path
- **Years 1-2**: Master the full scope of FA responsibilities
- **Years 3-4**: Transition to Chief of Staff role with expanded leadership responsibilities
- **Years 5+**: Potential progression to COO or other senior operational roles

## Work Environment
- **Flexibility**: Hybrid work arrangement with travel as needed
- **Pace**: Fast-paced, dynamic environment with varied daily responsibilities
- **Growth**: Significant opportunity for professional development and skill building
- **Impact**: Direct visibility into and influence on company strategy and operations
