# Salary Analysis - Founders Associate Role

## Market Research Summary (London, 2025)

### Role Benchmarking
Based on comprehensive market research of London salaries for hybrid executive support roles:

| Role Type | Salary Range | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Executive Assistant | £40,000 - £50,000 | Senior level, CEO support |
| Founders Associate | £35,000 - £50,000 | Traditional startup FA role |
| Chief of Staff | £70,000 - £100,000 | Strategic leadership role |
| **Our Hybrid Role** | **£55,000 - £70,000** | **FA + Strategic component** |

### Market Positioning
Our role sits between traditional FA and Chief of Staff:
- **30% Executive Support** (EA-level tasks)
- **35% Strategic Projects** (CoS-level work)
- **25% Company Operations** (Business operations)
- **10% Personal Support** (Lifestyle management)

### Recommended Compensation Framework

**Target Package:**
- **Base Salary**: £60,000
- **Equity**: 0.5% starting (scalable to 2% for long-term retention)
- **Benefits**: Standard UK package + travel/expenses
- **Total Package Value**: ~£65,000-£70,000 equivalent

### Justification for £60K Base
1. **Strategic Component**: 35% of role involves CoS-level work
2. **Broad Scope**: Company + personal support commands premium
3. **Direct Founder Access**: High-trust role with significant autonomy
4. **Career Progression**: Clear path to £70K-£100K CoS role
5. **London Premium**: 15-20% above UK average for similar roles

## Equity Considerations
- **Industry Standard**: 0.1-0.5% typical for senior operational roles
- **Retention Tool**: Vesting schedule incentivizes long-term commitment
- **Growth Potential**: Scalable to 2% for progression to CoS/COO

## Benefits Package
- **Standard UK Benefits**: Pension, healthcare, 25+ days holiday
- **Role-Specific**: Travel expenses, professional development
- **Flexibility**: Hybrid working arrangement
- **Growth Investment**: Training budget for skill development

## Market Competitiveness
The £60K + 0.5% equity package positions us:
- **Above traditional FA roles** (justifying strategic component)
- **Below full CoS roles** (appropriate for experience level)
- **Competitive with similar hybrid roles** in London market
- **Attractive progression pathway** to higher compensation

## Total Compensation Philosophy
Focus on total package value rather than just base salary:
- Competitive base for London market
- Equity upside for company growth
- Clear progression pathway
- Comprehensive benefits and flexibility
